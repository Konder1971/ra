About

We provide solutions that are adaptable, easy to use, and time sensitive

Rightapplications is a global digital technology studio with a team of passionate hand-picked professionals. We offer IT consultation, custom application/software development, enterprise IT solution, and UI/UX design. We provide a wide range of services and engagement models to ensure a variety of ways to best serve our clients. We work with founders and product owners in a wide range of industries; so, you can rest assured our team has the expertise required to help you build the right product for your target users. We also assist our clients in achieving their business objectives by outsourcing their non-core business processes in the areas of technology and digital marketing. We work very closely with our clients, helping them beyond product development. We develop custom mobile apps, web apps, and web-based applications; and make all the pieces work together. We are experts in developing complex applications that typically need to be integrated with other systems or different APIs and complex datasets.

Rightapplications was established in 2011 and operates from 2 offices: New York City (USA) and Eastern Europe (Ukraine).
